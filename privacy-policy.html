Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 27, 2026

Introduction

Thank you for choosing ScanID. This Privacy Policy explains how ScanID handles your information when you use our application.

Your privacy is important to us. ScanID is designed with a privacy-first approach, and all document processing is performed locally on your Android device.

Information We Collect

ScanID does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal information.

The application does not require:

  • User accounts
  • Registration
  • Login
  • Cloud storage
  • Internet connectivity

Photos and Documents

The application requests access to photos stored on your device solely to allow you to:

  • Select identity card images
  • Crop and edit images
  • Enhance image quality
  • Create A4 layouts
  • Generate PDF documents
  • Save processed images
  • Share exported files

All processing takes place entirely on your device.

Your photos and documents are never uploaded to any server.

Permissions

Depending on your Android version, ScanID may request:

  • Photos / Media access
  • Storage access
  • Camera access (optional, if you choose to capture images)

These permissions are used only for the features you choose to use.

Internet Usage

ScanID is designed to function completely offline.

The application does not transmit your files or personal information over the internet.

Advertising and Analytics

ScanID does not display advertisements.

ScanID does not use analytics or tracking services.

No advertising identifiers are collected.

Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Sell user information
  • Share user information with third parties
  • Upload files to cloud servers
  • Collect personal information

Data Security

Since all processing occurs locally, your files remain under your control.

We recommend protecting your device using a screen lock and other security features provided by Android.

Children's Privacy

ScanID does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any future changes will be posted on this page with an updated Effective Date.
